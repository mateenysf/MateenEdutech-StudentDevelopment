Policy Alignment

NEP 2020 and NCF 2023 call for students to become self-regulated, lifelong learners — able to plan, monitor, and reflect on their own learning. StudyFlow operationalises this vision through daily habit tracking, reflective journaling, and progress visualisation. The app develops the metacognitive and self-management competencies assessed by PARAKH and promoted by NCERT's 21st-century skill frameworks.
